Apples are a round, red and juicy fruit that is perfectly crunchy and delicious. An apple is an edible fruit that comes from an apple tree. There are thousands of varieties of apple trees worldwide. They contain no fat or sodium, have no cholesterol, and are an excellent source of fiber. They are related to roses and symbolize antagonists in movies and literature. There are 7,500 varieties of apples grown worldwide. Apples ripen faster at room temperature than when refrigerated.
